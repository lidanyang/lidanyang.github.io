¶494. Target Sum

Difficulty: Medium

You are given a list of non-negative integers, a1, a2, …, an, and a target, S. Now you have 2 symbols + and -. For each integer, you should choose one from + and - as its new symbol.

Find out how many ways to assign symbols to make sum of integers equal to target S.

Example 1:

Note:

  1. The length of the given array is positive and will not exceed 20.
  2. The sum of elements in the given array will not exceed 1000.
  3. Your output answer is guaranteed to be fitted in a 32-bit integer.

¶Solution

  首先，添加正负号后得到的式子和数组的总和的奇偶性相同。然后把所有正号对应的数的和记为S1，所有负号对应的数的和记为S2，则题目要求等价于S1-S2=S, S1+S2=sum，即S2=(sum-S)/2，即相当于求数组中和等于S2的子序列的个数。

¶790. Domino and Tromino Tiling

Difficulty: Medium

We have two types of tiles: a 2x1 domino shape, and an “L” tromino shape. These shapes may be rotated.

Given N, how many ways are there to tile a 2 x N board? Return your answer modulo 10^9 + 7.

(In a tiling, every square must be covered by a tile. Two tilings are different if and only if there are two 4-directionally adjacent cells on the board such that exactly one of the tilings has both squares occupied by a tile.)

Note:

  • N  will be in range [1, 1000].

¶Solution

¶法一

  分别下方和上方块数相同dp, 求上方比下方多一块dp1，下方比上方多一块dp2的摆法的个数，动态规划递推式为dp[i]=(dp[i-2]+dp[i-1]+dp1[i-1]+dp2[i-1]);dp1[i]=(dp[i-2]+dp2[i-1]);dp2[i]=(dp[i-2]+dp1[i-1]);

¶法二

  根据法一可以推出dp[i]=dp[i-1]*2+dp[i-3]。证明:将dp1[i]=(dp[i-2]+dp2[i-1])和dp2[i]=(dp[i-2]+dp1[i-1])代入dp[i]=(dp[i-2]+dp[i-1]+dp1[i-1]+dp2[i-1])可得dp[i]=dp[i-2]+dp[i-1]+dp1[i-1]+dp2[i-1]=dp[i-2]+dp[i-1]+dp[i-3]+dp2[i-2]+dp[i-3]+dp1[i-2]=dp[i-3]+dp[i-1]+(dp[i-3]+dp[i-2]+dp1[i-2]+dp2[i-2])=dp[i-3]+dp[i-1]+dp[i-1]=2*dp[i-1]+dp[i-3].

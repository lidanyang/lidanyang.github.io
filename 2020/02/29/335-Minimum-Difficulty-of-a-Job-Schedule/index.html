¶1335. Minimum Difficulty of a Job Schedule

Difficulty: Hard

You want to schedule a list of jobs in d days. Jobs are dependent (i.e To work on the i-th job, you have to finish all the jobs j where 0 <= j < i).

You have to finish at least one task every day. The difficulty of a job schedule is the sum of difficulties of each day of the d days. The difficulty of a day is the maximum difficulty of a job done in that day.

Given an array of integers jobDifficulty and an integer d. The difficulty of the i-th job is jobDifficulty[i].

Return the minimum difficulty of a job schedule. If you cannot find a schedule for the jobs return -1.

  在由前一天求后一天时，有很多重复计算。比如假设dp、dp1分别存放前一天和后一天的结果，递推式为dp1[i] = min(dp[j-1]+max(jobs[j]~jobs[i]))。在求dp1[i]的时候，如果jobs[i]之前第一个比jobs[i]大的值为jobs[k]，这时递推式中的max(jobs[j]~jobs[i])这一项就取决于jobs[k]，并且之后的最大值肯定不会再取jobs[i]，相当于递推式变成dp1[i] = min(min(dp[j1-1]+max(jobs[j1]~jobs[k])),min(dp[j2-1]+max(jobs[j2]~jobs[i])))=min(dp[k],min(dp[j2-1]+max(jobs[j2]~jobs[i])))，其中j1,j2满足j1 <= k < j2 <= i，其中用到了之前求过的dp[k]，jobs[k]是之前的第一个大于jobs[i]的值，这里出现了单调栈的使用场景即求数组中第一个大于某数的数。又因为jobs[k]是第一个大于jobs[i]的值，所以jobs[j2]肯定都小于jobs[i]，所以递推式中的min(dp[j2-1]+max(jobs[j2]~jobs[i]))可以进一步化简成min(dp[j2-1])+jobs[i]，综上dp1[i] = min( dp[k] , min(dp[j2-1]) + jobs[i])，i>=j2>k。
